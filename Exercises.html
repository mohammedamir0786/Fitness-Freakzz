Chest Exercises Description:

Chest exercises target the muscles of the chest,
primarily the pectoralis major and pectoralis minor.
Here are some common chest exercises:

  • Push-ups: Start in a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart.
    Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the ground,
    then push back up.
  • Bench Press: Lie on a flat bench with a barbell or dumbbells
    . Lower the weight to your chest,
    then press it back up.
  • Dumbbell Flyes: Lie on a bench with dumbbells held directly above your chest.
    Lower the weights out to the sides in a wide arc,
    then bring them back up.

Shoulder Exercises

Watch on YouTube

Shoulder Exercises Description:

Shoulder exercises help strengthen the deltoid muscles,
which consist of three heads: anterior, lateral,
and posterior. Here are some effective shoulder exercises:

  • Shoulder Press: Stand or sit with dumbbells or a barbell at shoulder height.
    Press the weight overhead until your arms are fully extended,
    then lower it back down.
  • Lateral Raises: Stand with dumbbells at your sides.
    Lift the weights out to the sides until they reach shoulder height,
    then lower them back down.
  • Front Raises: Hold dumbbells in front of your thighs with palms facing down.
    Lift the weights directly in front of you until they reach shoulder height,
    then lower them back down.
